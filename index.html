About

I live in Seattle and run my own company, Hunter Technologies, where I build commercial software applications. Occasionally, I take on high-impact consulting projects: short term, surgical strikes with startups solving difficult technical problems.

My roots are in Istanbul, Turkey, where I was born and raised, and where I attended Istanbul Erkek. I did my undergrad in Computer Science at Emory University in Atlanta.
